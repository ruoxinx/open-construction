OpenConstruction Overview

A centralized, carefully maintained catalog of open-access visual datasets and models for construction monitoring, safety, and productivity—designed to accelerate reproducible research and practical AI deployment.

About

OpenConstruction

OpenConstruction is a curated collection of publicly available datasets and models focused on construction monitoring and analysis. This serves as a central hub for researchers, practitioners, and developers on AI applications in the construction industry.

Vision

Why we built this

The industry is rapidly adopting AI-driven computer vision for monitoring, safety, and efficiency.

  • Provide a comprehensive catalog of open construction datasets
  • Standardize metadata for comparison and selection
  • Enable fair benchmarking and evaluation
  • Support reproducible research and education

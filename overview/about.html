About

OpenConstruction

OpenConstruction is a curated collection of publicly available datasets focused on construction monitoring and analysis. This serves as a central hub for researchers, practitioners, and developers working on AI applications in the construction industry.

Vision

Why we built this

The industry is rapidly adopting AI-driven computer vision for monitoring, safety, and efficiency. Effective models need high-quality, diverse datasets. Our goals:

  • Provide a comprehensive catalog of open construction datasets
  • Standardize metadata for comparison and selection
  • Enable fair benchmarking and evaluation
  • Support reproducible research and education

OpenConstruction is a **community-governed, open-source platform**.
We welcome contributions that improve both **the cataloged resources**
and **the platform infrastructure**.

---

## Ways to Contribute

You may contribute by:

- Adding or updating datasets, models, workflows, or OER entries
- Improving metadata schemas or validation scripts
- Developing community-maintained modules (ingestion, evaluators, benchmarks)
- Improving documentation, onboarding, or tooling

---

## Contribution Workflow

1. Open an issue using the appropriate template.
2. Fork the repository and create a feature branch.
3. Add or modify files following schema and contribution standards.
4. Submit a pull request (PR) describing your changes.
5. Address review feedback; once approved, the PR is merged and credited.

All contributions undergo **peer review** and **automated CI checks**.

---

## Quality & Review Criteria

Contributions are evaluated for:

- Schema compliance and metadata completeness
- Licensing clarity and attribution
- Ethical and legal considerations (e.g., privacy, consent)
- Overall quality and usefulness

Breaking changes must be clearly labeled and discussed in advance.

---

## Platform Modules

Contributors may develop **platform modules**, such as:

- Dataset ingestion helpers
- Model evaluation scaffolds
- Benchmark definitions
- Validation and enrichment utilities

Modules are registered in `modules/registry.json` and maintained by the community.

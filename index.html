About

What is OpenConstruction?

OpenConstruction is a curated catalog of open datasets, AI models, and use cases for the construction domain. We standardize metadata and links to speed discovery, reuse, and reproducibility across the project lifecycle.

Vision

Our Mission

We foster an open-science ecosystem by:

  • Maintaining an open catalog of construction AI resources.
  • Supporting research, education, and deployment.
  • Building a community-driven space for sharing and feedback.
Get Involved

Build the OpenConstruction Community

Open science thrives when diverse voices collaborate and work together. We invite you to participate in the OpenConstruction community. Here are a few ways to contribute:

  • Share resources: Submit new datasets or models or use cases to enrich the catalog.
  • Discuss & collaborate: Participate in our public forums and discussion.
  • Give feedback: Suggest features, report issues, and help prioritize.
  • Spread the word: Share the catalog to grow the community.
